INTORDUCTION

[Internet infrastructure] is an array of remote hardware, software and wetware working to send and receive information to/from various systems. Every instance of a page loading on a computer's web browser, every browser advertisement squashing into your screen, a Really Simple Syndication (RSS) feed streaming into a reader, or a Voice over Internet Protocol (VoIP) call being made represents this complex internet infrastructure. Its basic components include data centers, networks, servers, storage devices, server applications, and the most important, users in front of their monitors.

[Deepsea] is the lowest layer in the ocean, existing below the thermocline and above the seabed, at a depth of 1000 fathoms (1800 m) or more. Little or no light penetrates this part of the ocean and most of the organisms that live there rely for subsistence on falling organic matter produced in the photic zone. For this reason scientists once assumed that life would be sparse in the deep ocean but virtually every probe has revealed that, on the contrary, life is abundant in the deep ocean.

[Anthropology] is the study of homo sapiens, past and present, that draws and builds upon knowledge from the social sciences and biological sciences, as well as the humanities and the natural sciences.

It is essential to emphasize beforehand that since deepsea homo sapiens has been isolated far from continental homo sapiens, consequently, they varies a lot both biologically and culturally. Traditional anthropology research methods are no longer valid under such circumstance. Therefore, a new discipline - which is Deepsea Anthropology- must be founded in order to have a better understanding of this new homo sapiens subspecies, culturally and aesthetically within our current research scope.

The 4th volume of IIADA mainly focuses on an ancient witchcraft named Deepsea Arcana, which was originated in the 2000s, and operates based on the arts of “share”[1]. Since the ancient witchcraft and religions are the theoretical principal of the Deep Sea Arcana, at that time, it is believed by deepsea homo sapiens that by forwarding the portraits of certain deities, good luck will be delivered while evil be perished. The more sharing there is, the wider the blessing will spread.

[1] A long-sunk technique used by the deepsea homo sapiens of the 21st century to share information with other individuals through social network service.

前  言

【互联网基础建设】是一家集硬件，软件是远程控制湿件作品将是接收信息/ EMI方案。页面加载在您的计算机的Web浏览器的每个实例，每个浏览器的广告摔破到屏幕，真正简单聚合（RSS）进料流读卡器或互联网协议语音（VoIP）呼叫来表示这个复杂的互联网基础设施。其基本组成部分包括数据中心，网络，服务器，存储设备，服务器应用是关键的用户在他们的屏幕的前面。

【深海】是底层的大海，跃层下方和当前底在1000米（1,800米）或更大的深度。很少或没有光线穿过这片海域，和大多数生物的跌幅取决于由有机材料层产生的光的生计。因此，科学家们曾经认为生活会有点深海，但几乎所有的探测结果表明，与此相反，生活是丰富的深海。

【人类学】是研究智人，过去和现在，这吸引并提请注意社会和生物科学，以及人文和自然科学的研究。

第四卷主要集中在一个古老的巫术名为——深海秘术。[深海秘术]起源于21世纪00年代，操作原理基于“转发”[1]，由于古代巫术和宗教是深海阿卡纳的理论校长。在当时，它被认为是通过深海人类通过转发某些神的画像，好运将交付而被邪恶灭亡。共享越多有，更广泛的祝福会蔓延。

[1]使用21世纪的深海人类长期沉没的技术分享与通过社交网络服务的其他个人信息。
